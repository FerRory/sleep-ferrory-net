Sleep Tracker

Information

Sleep Tracker lets you save your mood and amount of sleep per day. This way you can keep track of your sleep patterns and how they affect your mood. Data is stored locally on your device and is not shared with anyone. You can use Sleep Tracker offline and your data will remain on your device. If you clear your browser data, your saved moods and sleep data will be lost. The last 7 days will be saved.
